Introduction
Kick HLS Yardımcısı ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application.

Information We Collect
Google User Data
Our application uses Google OAuth services to access your YouTube account. We request access to the following data:

YouTube Channel Information (Read-only): To display your channel name and avatar in the application interface.
YouTube Videos (Upload): To allow you to upload video files directly from your computer to your YouTube channel.
We do not store your personal data on our servers. All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device in the application's settings file (settings.json).

Local Data
We store the following information locally on your device:

Application settings (e.g., default download folder).
Authentication tokens (access and refresh tokens) for YouTube.
How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect solely to provide the functionality of the application:

To authenticate you with YouTube.
To display your login status.
To upload videos you select to your YouTube channel.
Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or disclose your Google User Data to third parties. Your data is transferred directly between your device and Google's servers via the official YouTube API.

Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Your authentication tokens are stored locally on your machine. We recommend keeping your computer secure to prevent unauthorized access to these tokens.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
